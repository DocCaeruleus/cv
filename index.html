Sebastian Kaj-Joakim Landor

Ph.D. in medical science, M.Sc. in Biochemistry

I am a biochemist/cell biologist with 10+ years of experience in basic oncology research, with focus on Notch signalling in breast cancer and post-translational modifications (PTMs) of Notch. Special interest in PTMs, cancer immunotherapy, and aging research. Proficiency in most wet lab and microscopy techniques. A critical thinker inspired by technology and translational science, as well as lifespan and healthspan improvements.
